# Yu-Gi-Oh Favorite Cards Challenge

A simple interactive template that lets Yu-Gi-Oh players create and share their **favorite cards by category**.

Upload your cards, generate the infographic, and share your Yu-Gi-Oh journey with the community.

## Preview

Create your own infographic with categories such as:

- Favorite Deck
- Normal Monster
- Effect Monster
- Ritual Monster
- Fusion Monster
- Synchro Monster
- Xyz Monster
- Pendulum Monster
- Link Monster

Then download the final image and share it on social media.

## How it works

1. Click on a card slot.
2. Upload the image of your favorite card.
3. Repeat for all categories.
4. Click **Download my Infographic**.
5. Share your result on Facebook, Twitter, Reddit or Discord.

The image will automatically include the template layout.

## Features

- Simple and clean Yu-Gi-Oh themed design
- Upload your own card images
- Automatic infographic generation
- High resolution image export
- Works directly in the browser
- No account required
- No data stored
